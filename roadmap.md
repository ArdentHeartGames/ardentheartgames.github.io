# Development Roadmap

Our high-level roadmap for the Ardent Heart Editor. This roadmap is subject to change based on community feedback and development priorities.

## Current Status: Pre-Early Access Development

We are currently in active development, working towards our Early Access launch on Steam. The editor is functional for 2D game development with core features implemented.

**Early Access Launch**: Q1 2026 (could be sooner)
**Current Access**: Invite-only until Early Access launch

## Release Schedule

We follow an iterative development approach:

- **Weekly**: Internal builds and testing
- **Monthly**: Feature milestones and improvements
- **Quarterly**: Major updates and new capabilities

### Key Milestones

1. **Early Access Launch** - Core 2D editor (Q1 2026 or sooner)
2. **Visual Editors Update** - Animation and scripting tools (Q2-Q3 2026)
3. **3D Integration** - ThreeJS support (Q4 2026 - Q1 2027)
4. **AI Features** - Local LLM integration (2027)
5. **Version 1.0** - Full featured release (2027+)

*Note: Dates are approximate and subject to change*
